Confidence Calculation
STEP 1
Base confidence
Count tiers whose avg sign matches the overall direction. Divide by 5.
NEUTRAL: count tiers with |avg| ≤ 0.10
STEP 2
VIX modifier
× 1.10 (LOW)
× 1.00 (NORMAL)
× 0.85 (HIGH)
× 0.60 (EXTREME)
STEP 3
ADX modifier
× 1.15 if ADX > 30
× 1.00 if ADX 20–30
× 0.80 if ADX < 20
× 1.00 if ADX unavailable
STEP 4
Divergence penalty
If Tier 4 direction ≠ overall direction → × 0.80 commentary divergence penalty
Cap at 1.0
confidence = base × vix_modifier × adx_modifier [ × 0.80 if tier4 diverges ]
Labels: LOW < 0.41  ·  MEDIUM 0.41–0.70  ·  HIGH > 0.70
Calculation Detail
Max Pain Algorithm
used in Tier 1A for the max pain vs spot vote
For every strike K in the option chain:
payout(K) =
 Σ call_OI × max(0, K − S_call)
 + Σ put_OI × max(0, S_put − K) where S = each option's own strike (not spot). Max pain = K that minimises total payout.
Source: Upstox option chain · computed in UpstoxOptionChainClient.computeMaxPain()
